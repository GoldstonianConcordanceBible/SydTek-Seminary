# Purpose

The SydTek Seminary Minister Registry serves as the official verification system for individuals who have received credentials, ministry recognition, licensing, or ordination through SydTek Seminary.

The Registry exists to provide transparency, accountability, and public verification of ministerial credentials.

---

# Registry Objectives

The Minister Registry is designed to:

- Verify ministry credentials
- Verify ordination status
- Verify licensing status
- Maintain public transparency
- Preserve credential history
- Support digital credential verification
- Support future accreditation readiness
- Support optional blockchain credential verification

---

# Registry Principles

## Truth

Registry records shall accurately reflect the status of each minister.

---

## Transparency

Registry information shall be publicly verifiable whenever appropriate.

---

## Stewardship

Ministerial credentials are considered stewardship responsibilities rather than status symbols.

---

## Accountability

Registry participation requires adherence to Seminary standards and policies.

---

# Credential Categories

The Registry recognizes the following credential categories.

## Ministry Certificate Holder

Individuals who have completed approved ministry-related educational programs.

Examples:

- Biblical Studies Certificate
- Ministry Formation Certificate
- Ethiopian Canon Certificate
- Systematic Theology Certificate

---

## Licensed Minister

Individuals approved for ministry service through the Seminary licensing process.

Examples:

- Teaching ministry
- Outreach ministry
- Community ministry
- Educational ministry

---

## Ordained Minister

Individuals who have completed the Seminary Ordination Framework and received formal commissioning.

---

## Senior Minister

Individuals recognized for sustained ministry impact, leadership, scholarship, and service.

---

## Honorary Recognition

Special recognition may be granted for extraordinary contributions to ministry, education, scholarship, or Christian service.

Honorary recognition does not automatically grant ordination status.

---

# Registry Record Structure

Each Registry record may contain:

## Required Fields

- Registry ID
- Full Name
- Credential Level
- Credential Status
- Issue Date

---

## Optional Fields

- Ministry Area
- Portfolio URL
- Published Works
- Institutional Affiliation
- Biography
- Credential Verification Link
- Digital Credential Identifier
- On-Chain Credential Reference

---

# Credential Status Types

Registry entries may contain one of the following statuses.

## Active

Credential is valid and in good standing.

---

## Inactive

Credential remains recorded but is not currently active.

---

## Retired

Minister has voluntarily retired from active ministry.

---

## Suspended

Credential has been temporarily suspended.

---

## Revoked

Credential has been permanently revoked following review.

---

# Credential Verification

Verification methods may include:

- Public Registry Entry
- Credential Verification Number
- Digital Certificate
- QR Code Verification
- GitHub Verification Record
- Credential Verification Portal
- Optional Blockchain Verification
