**Division:** SydTek Seminary

**Status:** Draft

---

# Purpose

The Systematic Theology Program provides a structured study of Christian doctrine through Scripture, theological reflection, ministry application, and portfolio-based assessment.

The program serves:

- Ministry candidates
- Licensed ministers
- Ordination candidates
- Christian educators
- Independent learners
- Researchers
- SydTek Seminary certificate pathways

---

# Program Philosophy

Systematic Theology is the organized study of what Scripture teaches regarding God, humanity, salvation, the Church, and creation.

SydTek Seminary approaches theology through the principle:

**Fluency Not Exposure**

The goal is not merely exposure to doctrine but demonstrated understanding and application.

# Program Structure

The Systematic Theology curriculum is organized into twelve modules.

---

# Module I

## Introduction to Theology

Topics:

- What is theology?
- Why theology matters
- Sources of theology
- Faith and reason
- Christian worldview

# Module II

Revelation

Topics:

* General revelation
* Special revelation
* Scripture and revelation
* Knowledge of God

# Module III

Doctrine of Scripture

Topics:

* Inspiration
* Authority
* Canon
* Reliability
* Biblical interpretation

# Module IV

Doctrine of Humanity

Topics:

* Image of God
* Human nature
* Sin
* Responsibility
* Human purpose

# Module V

Doctrine of God

Topics:

* Divine attributes
* Holiness
* Love
* Justice
* Sovereignty

# Module VI

Doctrine of Christ

Topics:

* Messiah
* Incarnation
* Ministry
* Crucifixion
* Resurrection

# Module VII

Doctrine of the Holy Spirit

Topics:

* Personhood
* Spiritual gifts
* Sanctification
* Guidance
* Empowerment

# Module VIII

Doctrine of Salvation

Topics:

* Grace
* Faith
* Repentance
* Justification
* Sanctification

# Module IX

Doctrine of the Church

Topics:

* Purpose of the Church
* Discipleship
* Worship
* Fellowship
* Mission

# Module X

Ethiopian Canon Studies

Topics:

* Canon history
* Ethiopian Christianity
* Expanded canon literature
* Biblical literacy

# Module XI

Topics:

* Moral reasoning
* Stewardship
* Leadership
* Service
* Accountability

* Resurrection
* Judgment
* New Creation
* Hope
