# Purpose

The Senior Minister Track recognizes sustained ministry impact, long-term leadership, theological contribution, educational service, and demonstrated stewardship over time.

This recognition is intended for individuals who have established a record of faithful ministry, mentorship, scholarship, and leadership.

Senior Minister recognition represents one of the highest ministry distinctions within SydTek Seminary.

---

# Foundational Principle

**Faithfulness over fame.**

SydTek Seminary recognizes that enduring service, discipleship, teaching, and stewardship are more important than visibility, popularity, or titles.

Senior Minister recognition acknowledges a demonstrated ministry legacy.

---

# Eligibility Requirements

Candidates should demonstrate:

- Ordained Minister status or equivalent ministry standing
- Sustained ministry service
- Continued theological development
- Demonstrated leadership
- Significant contribution to Christian education or ministry
- Christian character and integrity

---

# Recommended Experience

Candidates should generally demonstrate:

- Five or more years of documented ministry service
- Evidence of leadership responsibility
- Teaching experience
- Mentorship experience
- Community impact
- Public ministry contribution

Exceptional candidates may be reviewed individually.
