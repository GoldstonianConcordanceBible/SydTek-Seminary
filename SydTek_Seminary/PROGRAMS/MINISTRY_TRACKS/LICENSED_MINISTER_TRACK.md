# Licensed Minister Track

**Program Code:** LMT-500  
**Track Type:** Ministry Recognition Pathway  
**Institution:** SydTek Seminary  
**Status:** Draft

---

# Purpose

The Licensed Minister Track provides a structured pathway for individuals seeking formal recognition for active ministry service.

Licensing recognizes demonstrated ministry participation, foundational theological competency, Christian character, and commitment to continued ministry development.

Licensing serves as a preparatory step for those pursuing long-term ministry service and future ordination.

---

# Ministry Philosophy

SydTek Seminary affirms:

**Ministry is demonstrated before it is recognized.**

Licensing acknowledges existing ministry activity and encourages continued growth in teaching, discipleship, service, and leadership.

---

# Eligibility Requirements

Candidates should demonstrate:

- Christian commitment
- Active ministry involvement
- Agreement with the Seminary Statement of Faith
- Commitment to ethical conduct
- Willingness to participate in portfolio review

---

# Recommended Educational Preparation

Candidates are encouraged to complete:

## Certificate in Biblical Studies

Recommended Areas:

- Scripture
- Biblical literacy
- Interpretation
- Theology

---

## Certificate in Ministry Formation

Recommended Areas:

- Teaching
- Leadership
- Discipleship
- Ministry service

# Ministry Evidence Requirements

Candidates should demonstrate active ministry involvement.

Acceptable evidence may include:

- Sermons
- Bible studies
- Church service
- Community service
- Teaching activities
- Educational ministry
- Digital ministry
- Christian content creation
- Outreach initiatives

---

# Portfolio Review Requirements

Portfolio submissions may include:

## Teaching Evidence

Examples:

- Sermons
- Lessons
- Courses
- Workshops

---

## Service Evidence

Examples:

- Ministry service
- Community service
- Volunteer activities

---

## Leadership Evidence

Examples:

- Group leadership
- Ministry coordination
- Educational leadership

---

## Personal Reflection

Candidates submit a written reflection discussing:

- Calling to ministry
- Ministry experiences
- Lessons learned
- Future ministry goals

---

# Statement of Faith Requirement

Candidates must affirm the SydTek Seminary Statement of Faith.

---

# Character Review

Candidates should demonstrate:

- Integrity
- Humility
- Accountability
- Service
- Respect for others

---

# Licensing Review Process

### Step 1

Portfolio submission.

### Step 2

Statement of Faith review.

### Step 3

Ministry evidence review.

### Step 4

Committee recommendation.

### Step 5

Licensing approval.
