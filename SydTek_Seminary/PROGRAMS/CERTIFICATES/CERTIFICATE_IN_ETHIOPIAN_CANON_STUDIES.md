# Program Purpose

The Certificate in Ethiopian Canon Studies introduces learners to the history, structure, theology, and significance of the 81-Book Ethiopian Canon.

The program is designed to increase biblical literacy, provide historical context for canon development, and equip students to engage with texts often overlooked in Western theological education.

---

# Program Outcomes

Upon completion, learners should be able to:

- Explain the history of biblical canon formation
- Describe the structure of the 81-Book Ethiopian Canon
- Identify major differences between canon traditions
- Discuss the role of Ethiopia in Christian history
- Analyze key themes found within expanded canon literature
- Demonstrate increased biblical literacy
- Apply Ethiopian Canon studies within ministry and educational contexts
