# Certificate in Digital Ministry

**Program Code:** CDM-400  
**Credential Type:** Certificate Program  
**Institution:** SydTek Seminary  
**Delivery Format:** Portfolio-Based / Online / Independent Study  
**Status:** Draft

---

# Program Purpose

The Certificate in Digital Ministry prepares learners to teach, disciple, publish, and serve through digital platforms.

This certificate recognizes that ministry now happens through sermons, videos, podcasts, websites, repositories, newsletters, livestreams, and digital learning communities.

---

# Program Outcomes

Upon completion, learners should be able to:

- Build a digital ministry portfolio
- Publish Scripture-centered content online
- Create sermons, Bible studies, or teachings for digital platforms
- Use YouTube, podcasts, newsletters, or websites for ministry
- Organize ministry evidence for credential review
- Practice ethical digital communication
- Support transparent credential verification
