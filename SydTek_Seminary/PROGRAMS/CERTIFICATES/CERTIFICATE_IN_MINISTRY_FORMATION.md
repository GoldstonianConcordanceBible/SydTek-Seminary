# Certificate in Ministry Formation

**Program Code:** CMF-200  
**Credential Type:** Certificate Program  
**Institution:** SydTek Seminary  
**Delivery Format:** Portfolio-Based / Online / Independent Study  
**Status:** Draft

---

# Program Purpose

The Certificate in Ministry Formation prepares individuals for practical Christian ministry through teaching, discipleship, leadership, service, communication, and ministry stewardship.

The program is designed for:

- Ministry leaders
- Church volunteers
- Bible teachers
- Future licensed ministers
- Future ordained ministers
- Community leaders
- Christian educators

---

# Program Outcomes

Upon completion, learners should be able to:

- Teach Scripture effectively
- Lead Bible studies
- Disciple others
- Demonstrate Christian leadership
- Communicate biblical principles clearly
- Serve within ministry environments
- Develop a ministry portfolio

---

# Certificate Requirements

Candidates must complete all required modules and submit portfolio evidence demonstrating ministry competency.

# Portfolio Requirements

Students must provide ministry evidence demonstrating practical application.

Examples include:

- Sermons
- Bible studies
- Ministry workshops
- Teaching videos
- Community service projects
- Church service
- Outreach activities
- Mentorship activities

---

# Assessment Model

SydTek Seminary follows the principle:

**Fluency Not Exposure**

Candidates are evaluated based on demonstrated ministry competency rather than content consumption alone.

Assessment methods may include:

- Portfolio review
- Teaching demonstrations
- Ministry reflections
- Leadership projects
- Service documentation
- Public presentations

---

# Ministry Experience Requirement

Candidates should document a minimum period of active ministry involvement through one or more of the following:

- Church ministry
- Community service
- Educational ministry
- Digital ministry
- Outreach initiatives
- Discipleship activities

---

# Recommended Progression

After completion, students may continue into:

- Licensed Minister Track
- Certificate in Digital Ministry
- Certificate in Ethiopian Canon Studies
- Ordination Track
