# Certificate in Biblical Studies

**Program Code:** CBS-100  
**Credential Type:** Certificate Program  
**Institution:** SydTek Seminary  
**Delivery Format:** Portfolio-Based / Online / Independent Study  
**Status:** Draft

---

# Program Purpose

The Certificate in Biblical Studies provides foundational instruction in Scripture, biblical interpretation, theology, and Christian discipleship.

The program is designed for:

- Ministry leaders
- Bible teachers
- Homeschool educators
- Independent learners
- Future ordination candidates
- Church volunteers
- Christian content creators

---

# Program Outcomes

Upon completion, learners should be able to:

- Explain the major themes of Scripture
- Navigate the Old and New Testaments
- Demonstrate biblical literacy
- Interpret Scripture responsibly
- Understand foundational Christian theology
- Apply biblical principles to daily life
- Support teaching and discipleship ministries

---

# Certificate Requirements

Candidates must successfully complete all required modules and submit portfolio evidence demonstrating competency.

---

# Required Courses

## BIB-101 Introduction to the Bible

Topics:

- What is Scripture?
- Biblical inspiration
- Biblical authority
- Biblical literacy foundations

---

## BIB-102 Old Testament Survey

Topics:

- Creation
- Patriarchs
- Exodus
- Kingdoms
- Prophets
- Wisdom literature

---

## BIB-103 New Testament Survey

Topics:

- The Gospels
- Acts
- Pauline Epistles
- General Epistles
- Revelation

---

## BIB-104 Biblical Interpretation

Topics:

- Context
- Literary genres
- Historical background
- Observation
- Interpretation
- Application

---

## BIB-105 Biblical Theology

Topics:

- Covenant
- Kingdom
- Redemption
- Messiah
- Church
- Restoration

---

## BIB-106 Ethiopian Canon Foundations

Topics:

- Canon formation
- 81-Book Ethiopian Canon
- Canon history
- Comparative canon studies
