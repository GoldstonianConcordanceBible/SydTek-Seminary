# SydTek Seminary

SydTek Seminary is the theological education, ministry formation, ordination, and credentialing repository within the SydTek University and Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) ecosystem.

This repository exists to organize seminary governance, doctrine, curriculum, ordination standards, minister registry systems, digital credentials, and portfolio-based ministry formation.

---

## Purpose

SydTek Seminary exists to equip disciples, educators, ministers, researchers, and servant leaders through Scripture-centered education grounded in:

- Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB)
- Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine
- 81-Book Ethiopian Canon
- SydTek Scholars
- Fluency Not Exposure
